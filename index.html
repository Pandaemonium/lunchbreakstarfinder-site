What is it?

Think Brooklyn Nine‑Nine meets Deep Space Nine.

  • Weekly, self‑contained Starfinder one‑shots.
  • Designed for exactly 60 minutes of table time.
  • New‑player friendly; pre‑gens included.

What makes it unique?

  • Buzzers that resolve any scene on time.
  • Badges that reward how you solved it.
  • Recurring cast you’ll love to love (or roast).

How it runs in 60

The Lunch Break Recipe

0–5
Cold open, Dispatch ping, assign roles. Hand out Spotlight tokens.
5–20
Scene A: investigation/setup. 2 checks + 1 hazard or social beat.
20–35
Scene B: chase/puzzle/social duel. Start a 4‑segment clock.
35–50
Climax: 3‑round combat or contested skill challenge.
50–55
Resolution: consequences, twist, one Badge.
55–60
Buzzer: soft/medium/hard wrap to land on time.
